Hi! My name is Dalton Alves. I am a library and museum professional located in Maryland.

I presently work in Washington, DC in an academic special collections library. As a graduate student pursuing an MLIS at Simmons University, I have developed professional interests in libraries and special collections, digitization, art librarianship, and museums.

I have previously worked for the National Park Service in New Hampshire and at libraries and museums throughout Massachusetts.
